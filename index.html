About Us

Welcome to Circa Lagos, a contemporary restaurant and bar nestled in the vibrant heart of Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, Nigeria. Established five years ago with a passion for crafting unforgettable culinary experiences, we blend fresh, farm-to-table ingredients with innovative flavors to delight every palate.

At Circa Lagos, we pride ourselves on offering a refined yet relaxed atmosphere, perfect for intimate dinners, lively gatherings, or casual outings. Our menu features an array of premium dishes, from succulent seafood like our Seaman’s Pot and Seafood Fradiavolo, to hearty grilled meats such as Ram on Platter, creative pastas including Baked Pasta with chunky beef or spicy chicken, and indulgent desserts. Whether you're starting your day with breakfast or unwinding with signature cocktails at our bar, every bite and sip is designed to create lasting memories.
